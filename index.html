HELPS Society Activities

(Health Education Literacy Promoting Social Service Society). 

The HELPS is organized and will be operated exclusively for Educational, Health, Literacy promoting, Social Services for the needy (Orphans, Women, Children, Disabled & Old Age) and in furtherance thereof, specific objectives are adopted like (To promote an increased Education, Health & Social Services to the Needy (Orphans, Women, Children, Disabled & Old Age), To promote a greater interest in Social Service & Welfare Activities and its applications, To provide a means of communication between persons having interest in Social Service & Welfare Activities Additional objectives constitute every nook & corner that emphasizes the Development & welfare of the Needy & Poor).
To cooperate with government in all its activities to eradicate medical, health, family welfare and educational problems.
Provide health services to the rural and urban people, medical care, mother care, child care, family planning health education, prevention and control of diseases and other rehabilitation services and family counselling.
To promote agriculture developmental activities in backward-draught areas and literacy enhancement in farmers regarding various government schemes for their empowerment.
Development of handicapped, differently disabled persons in rural and urban areas.
Steps to eradicate water problems and initiative ideas to conserve water.
Youth empowering ideas by encouraging them in Indoor/outdoor sports, games, and personality development programs to serve the nation.
Competitive Coaching initiatives for backward students to get jobs and career guidance programs to increase Employment and reduce Unemployment ratios.
Mobile and Standard permanent library initiatives for education and literacy promotion in all nearby villages.
To visit orphanages, Old age homes for their need and strive for their basic needs and in future we try our best to establish homes respectively.
To promote economic balance of the society in all ways and strive for the rural backward people enhancement by obtaining contracts from central and state governments, public bodies or individuals or by executives and with the help of our society members.
Community halls establishment through our society.
Self-help groups for the rural mahila empowerment.
Programs to eradicate Disasters and Natural calamities and fight at the time of Natural and Accidental disasters.
Programs to maintain secularism among the people for national integration.
We strive for the people and society in all ways what we can do and help the government bodies in whatever ways they are in need.

"Services Rendering to the People"
Taking Different Initiatives to attract Investments in the Region where all Mandals were Draught declared and doesn’t have even one investment.
Taking Initiatives to create different types of Channels to Connect State and District Head Quarter in all ways like Education, Employment, Health and Other requirements for the People in region as it was Very backward and doesn’t have many Facilities.
Conducting Medical Health Camps for the Poor and Needy in Draught and Remote areas of the Constituency and around Kurnool District.
Conducting Camps on Water Conversation and effective use of it for farming and etc..,
Taking some initiatives to make region Friendly to setup Government and Private Institutions to attract different types of other related Investments.
Conducting different types of camps on Education, Literacy Promotion, and Public Policy Schemes in near villages by empowering them how to get public schemes in a hassle free way and what are the different schemes available for them etc..,
 Conducting Social Welfare camps to eradicate migration in Rural Backward areas and enlightening them with the new methods of cultivation to avoid crop loss and Schemes to Use to get benefit while they face loss.
Joining the Poor Students and Rejoining the Dropouts in near residential schools and counseling the remote villagers about compulsory education and benefits of Education who are facing lack of development in many ways due to no type of profession.
Taking initiatives in the name of Village Comeback those who are settled in towns and Cities due to worst conditions of region to come back to Villages and Participate in the Village Development and Empowerment Activities through different ways.

Our Agenda and Goals for HELPS SOCIETY

‘Health for All’ is our goal. A healthy society is an indicator of a developed society. Primary health is the vision behind the community-based health programme and it strives to achieve the following objectives. Improve the health of the community Create awareness and conduct health camps Promote primary healthcare at the community level Care and support for high risk groups Make low cost drugs available Develop a monitoring and evaluation mechanism to document the results Promote nutritional improvement schemes Provide preventive ,early warning and counseling services Encourage health insurance for all Networking of healthcare organizations Institute mid-day meals Improve hygiene through awareness programmes Provide sanitary facilities Proper waste disposal Environment awareness
